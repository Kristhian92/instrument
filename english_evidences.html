🔨 My Instrument: Painted Artist Shaker 🎨🪘

  • 1
    First, I collected one empty plastic bottle from my studio.
  • 2
    Then, I put rice inside the bottle to make sound.
  • 3
    Next, I closed the bottle tightly with their cap.
  • 4
    After that, I painted the bottle yellow, the color that represents Bucaramanga. 🎨
  • 5
    Finally, My artist shaker is ready to play and accompany any song. 🎉

🎶 How to Play It

To play the shaker, hold the bottle. Then, move it from side to side to create a rhythmic sound. You can move it quickly for a cheerful rhythm or slowly for a calm one.
